<b><size=20>Agent</size></b>

K_PathFinder.PathFinderAgent. You can find it in a PathFinder/Components folder. Currently it's do this:
1) Hold AgentProperties
2) Recieve your goals. Like "find path here".
3) Recieve result of Pathfinder work after it recieve your goals.
4) Serve as sort of wrapper to Path class
5) Serve as local avoidance agent
